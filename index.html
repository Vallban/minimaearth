Who are we?

Minima Earth was created by some online friends during March 2025, a joint work in which each of us has helped on something developing this project.

Regardless, those who play that where not here during the first days share some common values; all of us are leisurely Minecraft enjoyers that are additionally interested in diplomacy, estrategy and mpires roleplay
